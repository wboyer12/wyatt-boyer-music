Who is Wyatt Boyer?

Wyatt Boyer of Danville, IL pursues music in the styles of EDM, Hip-Hop, and Pop. On July 10th, 2020, he released his debut album “Welcome to Reality” featuring talented artists such as Crazy D. O., Zach Dable, Brooke Nelson, and more! He has expanded his style of producing with his acoustic release of "Do You Ever Feel" with Preston Fisher and Zane Simmons on December 18th, 2020. On June 4th, 2021, Wyatt Boyer released his song of the summer with Daniel Garzaro called, “Viaje Para Dos!” Since taking a step back from his own music, he has dedicated his creativity to produicng for other artists such as Daniel Garzaro with his song "C'est La Vie" and Frames with their songs "Reeling" and "Peach Pit." He continues his career in academics, photography, and music, as a student at Greenville University.
